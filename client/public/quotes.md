# Kenotic Landing Quotes

Three meditative quotes for the scrollable landing presentation.

## Quote 1: The Cross

"I asked the Lord to reveal Himself.

I asked to see Him more clearly. 

I asked to behold His divine nature.

Why is it that I am surprised to then stare at a cross?"

## Quote 2: The Kingdom

"

The Kingdom is already around us. Once you see it as a manifestation of Him, you cannot unsee it. 

Build no empire, feed the Kingdom.

"

**Source:** [The End of Empire, The Dawn of the Kingdom](/sermon/end-of-empire-dawn-of-kingdom)
